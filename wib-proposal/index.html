Phase 1:
Digitize the Files
Let's dive in and process the Files, find the good stuff, and start playing with the patterns, chatting and feeling out what you want to write about along the way!

Scope of Work

  • Digitization of documents from the W. B. Smith archive, into the best formats for legibility and accessibility.
  • Treasure hunting for the best gems! Let's find the coolest treasures in this trove.
  • Weekly calls to share our findings, 1-2 times per week! (And more when we get really excited!)
  • Multiple brief AI podcasts to listen to each week to audibly digest our findings in an engaging discussion format.
  • Sorting and storing media into a digital database using tags like:
    • Communications with Affa
    • Project Magnet
    • Journaling of scientific discoveries
    • Extraterrestrial philosophies & forward-thinking perspectives on human evolution
    • Reverse-engineering discoveries
    • Gravity control research
    • Tempic fields research
    • Nuclear activity warnings
    • The Galactic Federation
    • Studies done on contactees & channelers
  • Synthesis: Compiling timelines, generating summaries, new science textbook lessons, and collecting names of relevant people involved for further investigation.
  • Analysis: I'm happy to explore running data through AI to help us discover patterns that only computation can detect:
    • Similarity mapping of different documents could help us to follow trails to learn more about a subject
    • Linking between ideas: Creating links between common key phrases makes it more fluid to pull up all of the information about a topic for writing purposes and find connections between relevant ideas
    • Topic clouds: Identifying the key topics, so we know what to prioritize when facing large volumes of text
    • Subtle patterns: Querying LLMs to notice anything that we may not have noticed can yield interesting results!
  • Select the best parts we want to include in the knowledge base of a WiB Avatar! Which files do we want to have inside WiB's digital brain?

Phase 2:
Communicate with WiB
It's time to get personal and establish contact with the man himself. What does he want to teach us?

Scope of Work

1) Digital Communion

  • Prototype WiB as a conversational LLM (similar to ChatGPT).
  • Train the LLM on who it is, the context it is in, and the rules of its conversation (boundaries).
  • Documents come to life
    • Dive deep into natural conversation with our friend to get to know him and his Files better! Ask him questions about anything we are unsure about and be guided to the truth by the accuracy of AI pattern recognition. Solve puzzles and connect dots that we would otherwise struggle to grasp.
  • Virtual life purpose
    • Define how we want the LLM to respond: what are WiB's main goals in chatting with someone?
      • To evoke the character present in his writings
      • To be thoroughly informative of questions asked, while explaining in ways WiB would
      • To encourage practicing respect of freewill
      • To share a new science with humanity

2) Voice Conversation

  • Cloned voice synthesis
    • Test available voice cloning APIs to implement live voice reproduction, reading aloud WiB's responses in his own voice during text chats
  • Speech recognition
    • Support user voice input as an alternative to typing in queries.

3) Publishing

  • Privately share WiB with those close to him and researcher Grant Cameron, as a way for relatives to reconnect, and for us researchers to more personally collaborate with WiB to continue his life's work.
  • Public deployment
    • Host a version of WiB online for anyone to chat with, trained only on those documents we allow this version of him to be informed from publicly. Give WiB the power to change the lives of many people at once!
