    Presentation

Because I frequently follow discussions projecting the future on the Futurology subReddit, I was well aware of the discussion over the threat automation poses to the tradition of jobs, as well as the widely proposed basic income solution. I happily chose this topic, titling it 'Automated Income'. It aligns with many of my interests, such as automation, computer science, and artificial intelligence.
