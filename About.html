Who is Hunter?


Hunter Bobeck
VR Development
       

Hello there, I'm Hunter! As a developer, I reproduce, recombine, and remix the things that I am fascinated by. Lately I have become enamored with the virtual reality space.

Some people know me as Fel. I enjoy playing and creating games that push the limits of motion. I love to roleplay by going on sci-fi and fantasy adventures with others.

I've always been a dreamer – as a futurist, and literally. By practicing lucid dreaming, I like to test out VR mechanics when I sleep, then prototype them using my Moon Motion Toolkit!

It's for these reasons that VR is an exciting space to explore: we can move around indefinitely through the condensation of our imagination. That's why I first created dreamlike VR movement controls: so I can float around at ease in any virtual space.

In the meantime, I am finishing up my degree in Computer Science and Game Design at Indiana University (class of 2019), and always investigating new opportunities for VR, AR, AI, computers, art, music, stories, and games. I'm inspired by Alan Watts, Drizzt Do'Urden, & Yoda.
